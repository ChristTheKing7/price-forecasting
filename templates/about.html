Price4Cast is a project implemented by the students of makerere university of the a final year project

The project is intended to increase the accuracy of the existing forecasting models and to enhance explainability and transparency of of the predictions done by the machine learning algorithms

MISSION:
To provide the most accurate and Reliable price Trends and insights
VISION:
To enable a better way of life by enabling a better planning for both individuals and componies hence through our future price predictions

Price4Cast allows users to forecast prices for basic food commodities in different Ugandan markets. It is able to make recommendations to users about the best ideal procurement decissions and different time periods.

It provides Transparency for the machine learning algorithms in order to enable reliable predictions for the users.
